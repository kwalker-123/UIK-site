It's Science!

It has been scientifically proven that humans have a psychological tendency to produce postitive responses from interacting with our canaine friends. However in recent years, fMRI technology has allowed for further research into this response, and has shown that even just looking at a dog produces a smilar stres reducing effect.

It is thought that dogs being social animals, aswell as tens of thousands of years of co-evolution have given dogs and humans similar physiological responses in thier brains when looking at a loved one. They show the same empathatic concern and both humans and dogs recieve a boot in oxytocin (A hormone related to social bonding) levels when looking in one anothers eyes.
